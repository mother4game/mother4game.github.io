Update #1: Trailing Behind

Posted 12/25/2021

When three young boys discover an unwelcome guest deep within the woods, the entire county is turned on it's head! It's up to a small town teen named Aaron to discover the awesome power behind artifacts hidden deep within the earth known as Rift Shells before they're gone forever. On his world tour across podunk suburbia, arid deserts, and active factories, he makes many loyal friends, and out-there enemies. Though the presence of an untouchable entity who pulls the world's strings inspires the worst in dodgy people and deadly creatures, there's nothing to worry about with your friends behind your back!
